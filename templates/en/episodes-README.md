# episodes — episodic memory (recall units)

Holds the full lived experiences worth recalling — the complete scene of doing something once: why, how, which concepts, what happened. It's the story behind an experience lesson.

## What goes here
- When a finished roadmap item is **significant, and you might later need to revisit "what actually happened"** → save an episode.
- Most finished work is **not** kept here — distil it into an experience lesson and let the full scene fade (episodic memory decays by design).

## Relation to experience
experience = the lesson (a distilled index); episodes = the scene (the full experience). An experience lesson **links** to its episode — from the lesson you can return to the scene.

## Filenames
`YYYY-MM-DD-what-it-was.md`: date + one line of what it is.
